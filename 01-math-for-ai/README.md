# Mathematics For AI

## Why Mathematics Matters

AI models are built on mathematical principles.

Without mathematics, understanding AI becomes difficult.

## Linear Algebra

### Vectors

A vector is an ordered list of numbers.

Example:

[1,2,3]

### Matrices

A matrix is a table of numbers.

Example:

[[1,2],
 [3,4]]

Applications:

- Images
- Embeddings
- Neural Networks

## Calculus

Calculus helps optimize models.

### Derivative

Measures rate of change.

Example:

f(x)=x²

Derivative:

f'(x)=2x

Used in:

- Gradient Descent
- Backpropagation

## Probability

Probability measures uncertainty.

P(Event) = Favorable Outcomes / Total Outcomes

## Statistics

Important Concepts:

- Mean
- Median
- Mode
- Variance
- Standard Deviation

1. Multiply matrices manually.
2. Calculate derivatives.
3. Find variance of a dataset.
